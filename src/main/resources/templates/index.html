How It Works

A simple three-step flow to get your home service done.

1. Search

Register as a customer and search for a technician by service type and city.

2. Book

Select an approved provider and submit a booking with your preferred date and time.

3. Complete

After the job, share your OTP with the provider to confirm completion and leave a review.

Why Choose Rapid Reach?
